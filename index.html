FAQ

Common Questions

How much does it cost to partner with you?
Nothing. Zero. We invest in the launch — PPC, content, listing fees, logistics setup. You only supply the product at agreed wholesale pricing. No setup fee, no monthly retainer, no hidden charges.
What if my products are already being grey-sold in UAE?
This is exactly why you need us. We stop grey market sellers by securing official brand registry and exclusive distribution rights. We protect your pricing integrity and brand image on all Gulf marketplaces.
How long before we see first sales?
We aim for first sales within 30 days of the listing going live. Full momentum typically builds within 90 days. Our 1,000+ seller network provides immediate sales velocity.
Do I need to travel to UAE or KSA?
No. Everything is handled remotely. We manage the entire Gulf operation on your behalf — from customs clearance and warehousing to Amazon and Noon seller accounts, PPC, and monthly reporting.
How do my existing reviews transfer to Gulf listings?
Amazon allows review merging across marketplaces for the same ASIN. Your existing global reviews count towards Gulf listings — your brand launches with social proof already built in.
What categories do you work with?
Home & Kitchen, Beauty & Health, Sports & Fitness, Baby & Kids, Pet Products, Tools, Electronics accessories, and more.
What about customs and import?
We handle all UAE and KSA customs clearance and import documentation. You ship to our warehouse or use Amazon FBA — your choice. We manage all compliance and regulatory requirements.
